Research Topic

  • Hardware acceleration of neural network algorithms
    Deep learning is now widely used in a variety of domains, and brain-inspired neural computing like neural network (NN) is considered to be one of the most powerful applications of deep learning, which requires high computing capability and energy efficiency. My research focuses on using hardware accelerator, including FPGA, GPU, and emerging devices, to boost the speed and energy efficiency of NNs.

  • Published 34 papers and 2 patents. Cited by more than 800 in Google Scholar. H-index is 14, and H10-index is 18. Lixue Xia received the Bast Paper Nominations in DAC 2017 and ITC 2018.
